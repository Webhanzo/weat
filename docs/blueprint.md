# **App Name**: WeEat

## Core Features:

- Create New Order: Allow users to initiate a new group order by selecting a restaurant from a list of available options.
- Restaurant Selection: Enable users to browse restaurants with details such as menus and delivery fees.
- Add Items to Order: Enable users to add specific food items from the selected restaurant's menu to the group order.
- Collaborative Order Adding: Allow multiple users to simultaneously add their individual food selections to a shared group order.
- Automatic Cost Calculation: Automatically calculate each person's share of the total bill, including food costs and proportional delivery fees, after the order is finalized.
- Order History: Provide a history of past orders with details of items ordered and amounts paid by each participant.
- Intelligent Suggestion Tool: Generative AI suggests restaurants or dishes for a group order, based on dietary restrictions, budget, cuisines, popularity and user preferences, which users can use as a tool to refine the order.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Vivid red (#E63946) to evoke appetite and excitement.
- Background color: Soft beige (#F1FAEE), providing a neutral and warm base.
- Accent color: Turquoise (#457B9D) to provide contrast and highlight key interactive elements.
- Headline font: 'Poppins', a geometric sans-serif for a contemporary, precise feel.
- Body font: 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif to balance modernity with a little warmth or personality.
- Use clear and engaging icons to represent different cuisines and dietary options, enhancing the ease of navigation and food selection.
- Incorporate smooth, subtle transitions when adding items to an order or updating costs to enhance the user experience without being distracting.
